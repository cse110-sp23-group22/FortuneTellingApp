(static) adjustIndexForHorroscope(day, month)

returns the index corresponding to the sign in the horoscope.JSON file based on the input user birthday, using the hardcoded date values. The following is how signs are selected: Aries (March 21 – April 19) Taurus (April 20 – May 20) Gemini (May 21 – June 20) Cancer (June 21 – July 22) Leo (July 23 – August 22) Virgo (August 23 – September 22) Libra (September 23 – October 22) Scorpio (October 23 – November 21) Sagittarius (November 22 – December 21) Capricorn (December 22 – January 19) Aquarius (January 20 – February 18) Pisces (February 19 – March 20)
Parameters:

day int day of the user birthday
month int month of the user birthday
