Class: TransitionCanvas

TransitionCanvas()

An extension of the canvas with extra functionality for playing animations. The canvas covers the entire block it is contained in; it is intended to play between transitions of "states" for that block element in the DOM.

connectedCallback()

Upon attaching the element to the DOM, determine the duration of the transition from the attached attribute. Also, assign the linked audio element its source based on the sfx attribute. Finally, play only the first frame of the animation, so that something can appear on the screen.
